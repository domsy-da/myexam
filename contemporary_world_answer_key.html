The Contemporary World Examination - Answer Key

I. Enumeration (2 points each item)

  1. 1. What are the three indicators measured by the Global Connected Index (GCI)? Spatial Interconnection, Technological adoption, Economic flow
  2. 2. According to Scholte (2005), what are the four key shifts in society brought by globalization? Growth from transplanitary to supra-territorial connectivity, Shift from capitalism to hyper capitalism, Move from nationalism towards pluralism and hybridity, From rationalism towards knowledge reflexivity
  3. 3. Name the three types of economic flow. Global trading, Capital flow, Movement/Flow of People
  4. 4. List three types of movement/flow of people. Social migration, Travel and leisure, Political Expulsion
  5. 5. What are the four components of Structural Adjustment Policies (SAP)? Reduce spending for Social Services, Prioritize Repaying Debts, Increase Export, Remove Tariffs for import
  6. 6. List three types of capital flow. Commercial Loans, Official Loans, Foreign direct investment
  7. 7. What are the three international institutions mentioned in the document? World Bank, International Monetary Fund, World Trade Organization

II. Identification (2 points each)

  1. 1. The process of expanding socio-cultural and socio-ecological from national to international. Globalization
  2. 2. The perspective or approach that recognizes and navigates cultural differences and similarities across various cultures. Transcultural Level
  3. 3. The movement of trade, capital, and information. Globalization
  4. 4. The social link of people. Transplanitary
  5. 5. A type of capitalism characterized by rapid and intense global flow. Hyper capitalism
  6. 6. The existence of diverse groups and cultures. Pluralism
  7. 7. The process of examining one's own assumptions, beliefs, and biases. Knowledge reflexivity
  8. 8. The international organization that regulates and facilitates international trade. World Trade Organization
  9. 9. The international institution that provides short-term emergency loans and serves as a lender of last resort. International Monetary Fund
  10. 10. The international institution that facilitates the investment of capital for member countries and provides loans for large-scale projects. World Bank

III. Multiple Choice (2 points each)

  1. 1. What is the definition of globalization? b) The process of expanding socio-cultural and socio-ecological from national to international.
  2. 2. Which term refers to the connection of social and culture across the globe? c) Supra-territorial
  3. 3. Which of the following is a key shift in society from nationalism? c) Towards pluralism and hybridity
  4. 4. What does the term "Hybridity" mean in the context of globalization? c) A combination or blend
  5. 5. What is the main concern of global trading? c) Importing and exporting
  6. 6. What is the purpose of the World Trade Organization? c) To create roles for global trading and investment.
  7. 7. What does "Knowledge reflexivity" involve? c) Examining one's own assumptions, beliefs, and biases.
  8. 8. What is an example of an official loan? b) Money granted by rich countries to developing countries.
